Link with WhatsApp

Follow these steps to link your WhatsApp account:

  1. Open WhatsApp and go to the three dots
  2. Press "Link a device"
  3. Choose "Link with phone number" or "QR code"
  4. If you chose "QR code", scan the QR code displayed on this page
  5. If you chose "Link with phone number", enter the digit code generated on this page

Enter your WhatsApp phone number and click the link button to connect your account.
